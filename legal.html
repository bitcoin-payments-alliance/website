Privacy Policy

Overview

The Bitcoin Payments Alliance ("we", "us") operates the website bitcoinpaymentsalliance.org. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website.

Data collection

This website is a static site. We do not use cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics tools. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data through this website.

Hosting

This website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect technical data such as IP addresses in server logs as part of their infrastructure. For details, see Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Contact

If you contact us via email, we will process the data you provide solely to respond to your inquiry. We will not share your data with third parties.

Your rights

Under the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP), you have the right to access, rectify, or delete any personal data we may hold. Contact us at info@bitcoinpaymentsalliance.org.
