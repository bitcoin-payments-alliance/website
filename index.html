Mission

Bridging the gap between Bitcoin technology and everyday commerce.

Around the world, pioneering communities have proven that Bitcoin payments work in practice — at supermarkets, restaurants, and local shops. But these efforts remain isolated. Each community solves the same challenges independently: merchant onboarding, point-of-sale integration, volatility management, and regulatory compliance.

The Bitcoin Payments Alliance connects these communities to share knowledge, develop common standards, and accelerate adoption globally. Together, we can achieve what no single project can do alone.
